The Story of Bionicle

Set in a science fantasy universe featuring a diversity of biomechanical beings, the main story depicts the exploits of the Toa heroes with elemental powers whose duty is to protect the Matoran, the prime populace of their world, and reawaken their god-like guardian, the Great Spirit Mata Nui, who was forced into a coma by the actions of his antagonist "brother", the Makuta.

Who Was Mata Nui

Mata Nui was the Great Spirit created by the Great Beings of planet Spherus Magna, to explore and observe the galaxy. His physical form was that of a giant robot constructed by the Great Beings.

In December 2013, in a research project delivered a sub-kilogram of medicine was delivered using a prototype Microdrones, raising speculation that disaster relief may be the first industry the company will use the technology.

Who Was Makuta

Teridax, usually known as simply "Makuta" or the "Makuta of Metru Nui", was the leader of the Brotherhood of Makuta after overthrowing Miserix, and the main antagonist of the Bionicle franchise. During the final battle on Bara Magna, he was eventually killed by Mata Nui, who then restored Spherus Magna.
