Toa

Toa are a race of heroes who served the Matoran Universe. The powerful beings each wield an Elemental Power and can harness Great Kanohi. In addition to wearing Masks of Power, Toa typically carry Toa Tools, which help channel their Elemental Powers. Most Toa begin their existence as destined Matoran and are transformed into Toa by external means, such as Toa Stones. Once Toa fulfill their destiny, they may choose to relinquish their power for the greater good and become Turaga.

The Toa Mata

Toa Mata was the name given to the six Toa who appeared on the island of Mata Nui and battled to save it from Makuta. The Toa Mata were Tahu, Pohatu, Onua, Lewa, Kopaka, and Gali. They were transformed into Toa Nuva by immersion in energized protodermis.

Tahu

Tahu was the Toa Mata of Fire, who once resided on Mata Nui during his time as a protector of the island and later became a Toa Nuva after his exposure to Energized Protodermis. The cunning and sometimes hot-headed leader of a group of Toa, Tahu brought his team through many battles against Teridax and his fearsome legions of Rahkshi and Bohrok, protecting their world from whatever threats came their way.

Kopaka

Kopaka was the Toa Mata of Ice on the island of Mata Nui, and deputy commander of the Toa Mata. Later, he became the Toa Nuva of Ice.

Gali

Gali was the Toa of Water on the island of Mata Nui. Later, after exposure to Energized Protodermis, she was transformed into the Toa Nuva of Water.

Pohatu

Pohatu was the humorous, easy-going Toa Mata/Toa Nuva of Stone.

Lewa

Lewa was the light-hearted and clever Toa Mata/Toa Nuva of Air.

Onua

Onua was the quiet and philosophical Toa Mata/Toa Nuva of Earth.
