The Story of Bionicle

Set in a science fantasy universe featuring a diversity of biomechanical beings, the main story depicts the exploits of the Toa heroes with elemental powers whose duty is to protect the Matoran, the prime populace of their world, and reawaken their god-like guardian, the Great Spirit Mata Nui, who was forced into a coma by the actions of his antagonist "brother", the makuta.

Mata Nui

Mata Nui was the Great Spirit created by the Great Beings of planet Spherus Magna, to explore and observe the galaxy. His physical form was that of a giant robot constructed by the Great Beings.

Makuta

Teridax, usually known as simply "Makuta" or the "Makuta of Metru Nui", was the leader of the Brotherhood of Makuta after overthrowing Miserix, and the main antagonist of the Bionicle franchise. During the final battle on Bara Magna, he was eventually killed by Mata Nui, who then restored Spherus Magna.

The Great Beings

The Great Beings The Great Beings were mysterious members of the Glatorian species who had a shared love of creating. Some of their creations include:

  1. the Matoran Universe
  2. the Element Lords
  3. the Red Star
  4. the Kanohi Ignik

The individuals who would become Great Beings were originally members of various Spherus Magna tribes.

Solis Magna System
Solis Magna System was the solar system in which The BIONICLE took place. The sun of the solar system is called Solis Magna. It was located in the known Galactic Universe.
Spherus Magna
The planet of the Great Beings, containing the original source of Energized Protodermis in its core. During the Shattering, the planet was split into three parts.

The Three parts

  • Aqua Magna - A moon of Bara Magna, once the Great Sea on Spherus Magna.
  • Bara Magna - A desert planet and the largest of the three pieces of Spherus Magna.
  • Bota Magna - A jungle planet that orbited Bara Magna.
