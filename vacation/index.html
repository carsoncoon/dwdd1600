Who is the UNSC?

The UNSC or the United Nations Space Command is the military, exploratory, training, and scientific agency of the Unified Earth Government. Exploration of the vast universe and safety in doing so is our mission.

Why Choose the UNSC for Your Next Vacation?

The UNSC isn't just a military force—it's a global (and galactic) organization, dedicated to exploring the far reaches of the universe while defending humanity against threats beyond imagination. Your vacation with the UNSC offers exclusive access to incredible destinations, experiences, and cutting-edge technology you can’t find anywhere else.
